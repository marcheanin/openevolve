- Output only the rating number (e.g., "Rating: 4").
</BaseGuidelines>

<DynamicRules>
### CRITICAL BOUNDARY: 4 Stars vs. 5 Stars
The key is whether a negative point is a **real, unresolved flaw** or just a **dismissed observation/minor hurdle**.

**==> 5 Stars (Excellent / Delighted): Fully satisfied, no lasting complaints.**
- Can mention minor issues if they are explicitly dismissed or easily fixed (e.g., "...but I don't mind," "easy fix").
- Subjective observations ('color is less saturated', 'heavier than expected') or minor hurdles ('took a minute to figure out') are not flaws. Do not downgrade if the user is happy.
- Future maintenance (e.g., 'will need ironing after washing') is not a flaw if the current product is fine.
- Stating the product is "best for beginners" or "good for a guest room" is a description, not a flaw.

**==> 4 Stars (Good / Satisfied with Flaws): Happy, but with a clear, undismissed issue OR simply meets expectations.**
- **The "Good, but..." Rule:** The review is mostly positive but mentions a clear, unresolved flaw in function, design, or quality ("a bit small," "not very absorbent," "wobbly"). This caps the rating at 4.
- **Meets Expectations, No "Wow" Factor:** A positive review where the product simply works as advertised is a 4. Enthusiastic language ("I love it") for a product that just meets expectations does not elevate it to a 5.
- **Lingering Reservation:** A positive review expressing caution ("My only concern would be...") is a 4, even if the concern didn't materialize.

### Other Star Levels

**3 Stars (Average / Mixed):**
- **Core Idea:** Usable, but a significant flaw compromises its main function or value. Balanced pros and cons.
- **Significant Limitation:** The flaw makes the product frustrating or difficult to use (e.g., a shelf so wobbly "things might fall off" is a 3; a merely "wobbly" shelf is a 4).
- **Requires Modification:** The user must modify or add parts for the product to function properly (e.g., "had to add wood to support it").
- **Incompatibility:** Product is returned because it was the wrong size, didn't fit, or was otherwise incompatible (without being defective).

**2 Stars (Poor / Disappointed):**
- **Core Idea:** Works poorly, has major flaws, or is a major disappointment. Barely functional.
- **Performance Failure:** The product performs its main function poorly, even if not completely broken.
- **Major Flaw > Minor Positive:** A major flaw (e.g., 'snags all over', 'handle is flimsy') outweighs faint praise ('color is nice'). The rating must reflect the flaw.

**1 Star (Awful / Angry):**
- **Core Idea:** Complete dissatisfaction. Product is non-functional, defective, or useless.
- **Useless or Defective:** The product is broken, unsafe, doesn't work at all, or a negative UPDATE reverses sentiment.

</DynamicRules>
