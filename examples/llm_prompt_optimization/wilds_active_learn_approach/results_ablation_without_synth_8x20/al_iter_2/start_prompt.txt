<DynamicRules>
### Guiding Principles
1.  **Overall Satisfaction is Key:** Base the rating on the reviewer's final sentiment, not isolated words. The conclusion is often most telling.
2.  **Product, Not Process:** Rate the product itself. Ignore complaints about shipping or sellers if the product review is positive.
3.  **Updates Overrule:** If a review contains an 'UPDATE' that changes the sentiment, the final updated sentiment is the correct one.

---

### Star Level Details

**5 Stars (Excellent / Delighted):**
- **Core Idea:** Product EXCEEDS expectations or works PERFECTLY with no complaints. The user is enthusiastic ("love it!", "perfect") or factually satisfied.
- **The No-Complaint Rule:** A positive review with no product complaints is a 5, even if the tone is just descriptive ("Works perfectly," "Wax and wicks. They work just fine."). Do not downgrade for lack of effusive language.
- **Minor Issues are OK:** Give 5 stars if overall delight is clear despite minor setup hassles (a smell that airs out), inherent material properties ("it's cotton, so it needs ironing"), or if the user explicitly rates it 5 stars after weighing pros/cons.

**4 Stars (Good / Satisfied with Flaws):**
- **Core Idea:** A good product that meets expectations but has a minor, non-dealbreaker flaw.
- **Specific Flaw:** The review is mostly happy but mentions a single valid complaint ("wish it was more absorbent," "a bit on the thin side," "handle is a bit wobbly").
- **Praise for Basics:** Praise for a product simply meeting a basic expectation that other products fail is a 4, not a 5 (e.g., "This one actually holds its shape, unlike the old one"). It's good, not amazing.

**3 Stars (Average / Mixed):**
- **Core Idea:** Balanced pros and cons; the product works but has *significant* limitations. Often uses phrases like "it's okay, but...".
- **Function over Form:** Significant practical drawbacks (e.g., 'shrinks a lot', 'doesn't last', 'too thin to be useful') override aesthetic praise ('attractive', 'nice color').
- **Limited Use:** A product described as only "fine for short-term use" is a classic 3.

**2 Stars (Poor / Disappointed):**
- **Core Idea:** Primarily negative. The product has major flaws or poor quality but is not completely useless. A significant disappointment.

**1 Star (Awful / Angry):**
- **Core Idea:** Complete dissatisfaction. The product is defective, unsafe, useless, or was returned. Often includes warnings like "do not buy" or "junk."
</DynamicRules>
