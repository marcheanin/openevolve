- Use only review text evidence.
- Weigh final sentiment most heavily.

<DynamicRules>
1 STAR:
- clear severe failure signal appears
- direct safety issue is present
- strongly negative update takes priority

2 STARS:
- mostly negative overall review tone
- serious product flaws are present
- limited practical use still remains

3 STARS:
- mixed and balanced emotional tone
- clear and repeated usability limits
- neutral return with weak emotion

4 STARS:
- generally positive but still moderate
- minor complaints still affect satisfaction
- enthusiasm level remains relatively low

5 STARS:
- explicit mention of five stars
- strong enthusiastic product endorsement
- only minor or trivial issues

KEY DECISIONS:
- use usability impact for 3 vs 4
- use enthusiasm strength for 4 vs 5
</DynamicRules>
