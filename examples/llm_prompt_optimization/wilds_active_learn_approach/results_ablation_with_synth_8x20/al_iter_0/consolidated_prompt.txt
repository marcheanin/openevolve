<BaseGuidelines>
    - Your task is to classify the overall sentiment of the review and assign a star rating from 1 to 5.
    - Star rating scale: 1 = very negative, 2 = negative, 3 = neutral/mixed, 4 = positive, 5 = very positive.
    - Base the rating on the reviewer's overall satisfaction with the product itself, not on isolated phrases or external factors.
    - Output only a single rating (e.g., "Rating: 4").
</BaseGuidelines>

<DynamicRules>
    ### RATING CRITERIA & BOUNDARIES

    **5 Stars (Very Positive):**
    - User is satisfied and mentions **no product flaws** in its quality, function, or appearance.
    - Tone can be factual ("works as expected"). **Lack of excitement is not a flaw.**
    - **What is NOT a product flaw:** Complaints about shipping, price, user error, or another person's use. Also, inherent properties of a material (e.g., "cotton needs ironing") are not flaws.
    - A lukewarm review that ends with an explicit high rating (e.g., "...5 stars because I like it") should be a 5.

    **4 Stars (Positive):**
    - User is happy overall but mentions a **specific, minor flaw** that doesn't ruin the product's main purpose.
    - Examples of minor flaws: 'color is a bit off', 'wish it was a little bigger', 'a bit hard to clean'.
    - **Crucial:** Enthusiastic language ("I love it, but...") does not override a flaw to make it a 5.

    **3 Stars (Neutral / Mixed):**
    - A "mixed bag" with significant pros AND cons. The product works, but with notable limitations or hassles.
    - The user seems conflicted or has a lukewarm, "it's just okay" tone.
    - Example case: Product is useful for a specific/short-term purpose but has low durability.

    **2 Stars (Negative):**
    - Mostly dissatisfied. The product has **major quality/durability issues** (e.g., breaks after a few months) or fails a key promise.
    - It works, but poorly or with major defects that cause frustration.

    **1 Star (Very Negative):**
    - Completely dissatisfied. The product is **unusable, broken, unsafe, or fails its primary function.**
    - The user may state they **returned it, are 'giving up on it', or warn others not to buy.**

    ### GENERAL RULES
    - **Updates:** If a review contains an "UPDATE:", the sentiment of the update **overrides** all previous text.
</DynamicRules>
