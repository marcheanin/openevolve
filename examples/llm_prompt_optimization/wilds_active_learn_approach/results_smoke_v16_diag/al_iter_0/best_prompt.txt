<DynamicRules>
    STAR LEVEL CRITERIA:

    1 Star (Catastrophic Failure):
    - The product is completely non-functional, defective, unsafe, or arrived broken.
    - The user expresses strong anger or warns others not to buy.
    - Key indicators: "returned", "junk", "garbage", "dangerous", "fire hazard", "doesn't work at all".

    2 Stars (Major Flaws):
    - The product works, but poorly or with significant problems that cause major frustration.
    - There are serious discrepancies with the product description, missing parts, or very poor quality.
    - Key indicators: "disappointed", "a pain to use", "barely works", "poor quality", "not as described".

    3 Stars (Significant Trade-off):
    - The review contains a balance of significant pros and cons. The user has mixed feelings.
    - The product is usable but has notable limitations that prevent strong satisfaction.
    - Example: Looks great but functions poorly, or works well but has a major design flaw.
    - Key indicators: "it's okay", "decent for the price", "mixed feelings", "good and bad".

    4 Stars (Good, with Minor Issues):
    - The reviewer is happy and satisfied overall, but mentions a minor flaw, inconvenience, or something that could be better.
    - The product works well and meets expectations, but isn't flawless.
    - Key indicators: "pretty good", "works well but...", "my only complaint is...", "awesome" (when not implying perfection).

    5 Stars (Excellent / Perfect):
    - The reviewer is enthusiastic and has no significant reservations or complaints.
    - The product meets or exceeds all expectations. This includes simple products that perfectly fulfill their function without any issues, even if the review is short (e.g., 'Works perfectly', 'Just what I needed').
    - Key indicators: "perfect", "love it", "excellent", "flawless", "exceeded expectations", "highly recommend".

    GUIDING PRINCIPLES:

    - **4 vs 5 Stars:** A review with strong positive words ("love it") but also a clear, minor limitation ("wish it was bigger," "not microwave safe") is a 4. A 5-star review has no such reservations.
    - **Short & Simple Reviews:** For simple products, short positive statements like "They work!" or "Really cute" indicate satisfaction, not neutrality. In the absence of complaints, these are a 4 or 5.
    - **Holistic Tone:** Do not over-index on single words. A review calling a product "average" but then praising a "very smart idea" that makes it "Great for use" is likely a 5. The overall problem-solving value and satisfaction outweigh one neutral descriptor.
    - **Form vs. Function:** If a product looks good but works poorly, it's typically a 3 or lower. If it works great but has a minor aesthetic flaw, it's often a 4.

</DynamicRules>
