<DynamicRules>
    ### Star Level Definitions ###

    **5 Stars (Very Positive / Excellent):**
    - The user is delighted or highly satisfied. Keywords: "love," "perfect," "excellent," "amazing," "highly recommend."
    - The product meets or exceeds expectations. Give a 5 for flawless performance or for perfectly solving a stated problem (e.g., "finally found one that doesn't rust"), even without superlatives.
    - A review can still be a 5 if it mentions a tiny flaw but the user dismisses it and confirms high satisfaction (e.g., "cord is a bit short, but I'm very pleased").

    **4 Stars (Positive / Good):**
    - The user is satisfied, but with minor reservations, or expresses positive but not enthusiastic sentiment.
    - The product works well but has a minor flaw that doesn't undermine its main function.
    - Short, simple positive reviews are typically 4 stars (e.g., "It works," "No complaints," "Awesome pan!"). A 5-star rating requires more detail about *why* it was exceptional.
    - Purely descriptive reviews with no complaints (e.g., "The color is accurate and the material is sturdy") should be 4 stars.

    **3 Stars (Neutral / Mixed):**
    - The user has balanced feelings; the product has both significant pros and cons.
    - The product works, but with notable limitations that prevent a positive recommendation (e.g., "looks nice, but doesn't absorb water").
    - A 3-star rating requires substantial negative points to balance the positive ones. Do not use it for positive reviews that just lack enthusiasm.

    **2 Stars (Negative / Disappointing):**
    - The user is dissatisfied; negative aspects clearly outweigh the positive.
    - The product has significant functional flaws, is poor quality, or doesn't work as described. Keywords: "disappointed," "cheap," "pain to use," "would not recommend."

    **1 Star (Very Negative / Terrible):**
    - The user is angry or completely dissatisfied. The product is considered junk, non-functional, or dangerous.
    - Any mention of a safety hazard (e.g., "fire hazard," "dangerous") strongly indicates a 1-star rating.
    - Keywords: "do not buy," "garbage," "returned immediately." A product arriving broken or failing on first use is a 1.

</DynamicRules>
