<DynamicRules>
    ### Star Level Definitions ###

    **5 Stars (Very Positive / Excellent):**
    - The user is delighted or highly satisfied. Keywords: "love," "perfect," "excellent," "highly recommend."
    - The product meets or exceeds expectations. A review that states the product is "flawless" or "works perfectly" with no complaints is a 5.
    - **Crucially, a review that describes how the product solved a long-standing user problem (e.g., "finally found one that doesn't rust") is a 5-star review, even without superlatives.**
    - A review can still be a 5 if it mentions a tiny flaw but the user explicitly dismisses it and confirms high satisfaction (e.g., "cord is short, but that's a tiny issue for a machine this good").

    **4 Stars (Positive / Good):**
    - The user is satisfied, but with minor reservations, or expresses positive but not ecstatic sentiment.
    - The product works well but has a minor flaw that is mentioned and not dismissed.
    - Short, simple positive reviews with general praise are typically 4 stars (e.g., "It works," "No complaints," "Awesome pan!").
    - Reviews that focus primarily on aesthetics ("love the color," "beautiful design") but describe the function as merely adequate are 4 stars.

    **3 Stars (Neutral / Mixed):**
    - The user has balanced feelings; the product has both significant pros and cons.
    - The product works, but with notable limitations (e.g., "looks nice, but isn't functional").
    - A review mentioning a potential design flaw that could be a safety concern (e.g., "I can imagine your hand slipping") should be rated 3 stars, even if no accident occurred.

    **2 Stars (Negative / Disappointing):**
    - The user is dissatisfied; negative aspects clearly outweigh the positive.
    - The product has significant functional flaws, is poor quality, or doesn't work as described. Keywords: "disappointed," "cheap," "would not recommend."

    **1 Star (Very Negative / Terrible):**
    - The user is angry or completely dissatisfied. The product is considered junk, non-functional, or dangerous.
    - Any mention of a safety or health hazard (e.g., "fire hazard," "peeling into my food") is a 1-star rating.

</DynamicRules>
